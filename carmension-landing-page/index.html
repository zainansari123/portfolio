How It Works

Are you a car owner looking for a garage? Carmension helps you find local garages, with an easy to use interface, and rating system, so you can find a garage that suits you best.

If you are a garage owner, you can simply make an account, and start receiving service requests through Carmension.

Carmension Benefits

Carmension eliminates the struggle of finding the best garages with the cheapest prices by providing a dynamic platform. We love connecting garage businesses with people that need their services.

Welcome to Carmension

Where you will experience first-class simplicity, for your garage “needs”.

Awesome Features

Finding the right garage shouldn’t be that hard, it is not with Carmension.

  • Search & Filter

    Do you have any specific needs for your car? Maybe you want to find a garage in your city? Whatever your specific needs are, you can filter, and search, for the exact garage you need.

  • Who can find me

    As a garage business owner, you might want to control who can find your business, with Carmension you have full control over who can find, search, and view your business.

  • Chat Support

    Whenever you have an issue with something, you can get in touch with our support team, we are happy to help you.

  • Visibility

    As a garage business owner you want to be visible to as many clients as possible, with Carmension you can easily be searched, found, and viewed by potential customers.

  • Customized Offers

    Upload your special offers with imagery or videos, so you can get more customers your way.

WHAT IS Carmension

Carmension is an app for car garage business owners. Its users can find each other through the high-quality search engine, which is designed to make life easier for both parties.

About Us

We are a startup company based in Abu Dhabi, UAE. Our story started in 2017 when H.H. Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid, Vice President and Ruler of Dubai, announced the initiative to help train one million young Arabs in the language of the future, computer programming, and urged the Arab youth to take initiative and do not waste time and miss opportunities, new technologies will create new opportunities. And there we are creating opportunities, not only for us but for others as well.

Our mission is to bring vehicle users and service providers together. Our vision is to be the most used application in the UAE.

Carmension is a solution for people who search for garages and an opportunity for garage owners to list their services and offers to reach their voice to potential customers.

The application is user friendly, through subscription; garage owners will have full control of their accounts, can add, remove offers or modify services at any time.

Carmension is the link between solution/service seekers and service provider.
